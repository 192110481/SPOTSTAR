Nani

"Nani is a popular Indian actor, producer, and television presenter, known for his work in Telugu cinema. His full name is Ghanta Naveen Babu, and he is widely regarded for his natural acting style and versatility. Nani made his acting debut in the 2008 film Ashta Chamma and quickly rose to fame with his performances in movies such as Eega, Bhale Bhale Magadivoy, Nenu Local, and Jersey. "
