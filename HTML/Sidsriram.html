Sid Sriram

"Sid Sriram is an Indian-American playback singer and music composer known for his work in the Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Hindi film industries. He is recognized for his soulful voice and versatile singing style, often blending classical and contemporary music. Sid Sriram's career took off with his hit songs in Tamil and Telugu films, and he's become a prominent figure in the South Indian music industry."
