S. P. Balasubhramanyam

"S.P. Balasubrahmanyam, often referred to as SPB, was a legendary Indian playback singer, music director, and actor. He was born on June 4, 1946, in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh, and passed away on September 25, 2020. SPB is widely regarded as one of the most versatile and influential singers in the history of Indian music."
