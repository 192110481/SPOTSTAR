Shreya Ghoshal

"Shreya Ghoshal is an acclaimed Indian playback singer, known for her beautiful and versatile voice. She was born on March 12, 1984, in Murshidabad, West Bengal, India. Shreya has won numerous awards, including several National Film Awards and Filmfare Awards, and is celebrated for her work across various Indian languages, including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam."
