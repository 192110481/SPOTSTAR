Thaman S

"Thaman S is a prominent Indian music composer, singer, and music director, primarily known for his work in Telugu and Tamil cinema. He has composed music for several hit films in multiple languages, including Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, and Hindi.Thaman made his debut as a music director with the film "Sri" (2005), but his breakthrough came with "Killer" (2009). Over the years, he gained popularity for his energetic and catchy compositions."
