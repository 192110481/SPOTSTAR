Jr NTR

"Jr NTR, also known as Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., is a prominent Indian actor and a member of the Nandamuri family. He is one of the leading actors in Telugu cinema, known for his powerful performances and versatile roles. Jr NTR made his debut as a child artist in the film Brahmarshi Vishwamitra (1991) and later became a star with movies like Student No.1, Simhadri, Yamadonga, Temper, and RRR."
