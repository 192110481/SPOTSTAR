Pawan Kalyan

"Pawan Kalyan is an Indian actor, politician, and film producer known primarily for his work in Telugu cinema. He is often referred to as the 'Power Star' by his fans. Pawan made his acting debut in 1996 with the film Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi and gained widespread popularity with films like Tholi Prema, Badri, Gabbar Singh, Attarintiki Daredi, and Agnyaathavaasi."
