Ilaiyaraaja

"Ilaiyaraaja is a legendary Indian film composer, conductor, and songwriter, primarily known for his work in the Tamil film industry. He is often referred to as "Isaignani" (The Musical Genius). Ilaiyaraaja is widely regarded as one of the greatest composers in Indian cinema due to his innovative approach to music, blending classical, folk, and western styles."
