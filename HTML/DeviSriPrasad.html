Devi Sri Prasad

"Devi Sri Prasad, commonly known as DSP, is a renowned Indian music composer, playback singer, and music producer. He is predominantly known for his work in Telugu and Tamil cinema, but he has also worked in Hindi films. DSP is widely recognized for his energetic compositions, catchy tunes, and versatile music style that blends various genres, including folk, classical, and electronic music."
